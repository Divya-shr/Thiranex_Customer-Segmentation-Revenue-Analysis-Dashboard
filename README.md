# Customer Segmentation & Revenue Analysis Dashboard

## Project Overview

This project analyzes customer behavior and sales performance using Microsoft Power BI.

Customers are segmented into:
- Big Customers
- Medium Customers
- Small Customers

based on purchase value and revenue contribution.

## Objectives

- Perform customer segmentation
- Analyze purchase patterns
- Identify top-performing products
- Compare regional sales performance
- Generate business insights

## Tools Used

- Microsoft Power BI
- CSV Dataset
- Data Visualization
- Customer Analytics

## Dashboard Features

### KPIs
- Total Revenue
- Total Customers
- Total Orders

### Visualizations
- Customer Segment Distribution
- Revenue by Customer Segment
- Revenue by Category
- Revenue by Region
- Top 10 Products by Revenue

### Interactive Filters
- Region
- Category
- Customer Type

## Business Insights

- High-value customers contribute a significant share of revenue.
- Technology products show strong sales performance.
- Regional sales vary significantly.
- Customer segmentation helps target marketing campaigns effectively.

## Outcome

This dashboard provides actionable customer insights and supports data-driven business decisions.
